Project Research Fellowship (JRF)

Project:

Intelligent Test and Reliability Architecture for Extreme Low-power IoT Sensor Nodes

Problem to solve:

To develop a design for test (DfT) solution for online testing of IoT edge nodes.

Required knowledge:

If you have master in CS/EE and good understanding of microprocessor, verilog/vhdl, and C/C++/python coding and interested in solving problems, that would suffice for the project work. Knowledge in Testing of VLSI system would be a boost for the project work.

Expected outcome:

Online IoT test infrastructure, energy efficient test mechanism and algorithms, results of experimental analysis on power efficiency and test coverage.

Nature of Work:

IoT system building, Energy analysis of IoT node, Verilog/VHDL Coding, Fault Injection in FPGA board, ATPG and Fault simulation, BIST insertion and synthesis, Evaluation of DfT techniques for contemporary circuit etc. (Works involves C/C++/Python coding, use of CAD tools, and FPGA boards.)

Funding:

SERB, Govt of India

Contact:
